NIH Dietary Guidelines: DASH (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) eating plan: rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, low-fat dairy; includes fish, poultry, beans, nuts; limits saturated fat, sodium, sweets, sugary beverages, red meats. Heart-healthy eating: limit sodium to 1500-2300 mg/day, saturated fat < 7% of calories, trans fat as low as possible, dietary cholesterol < 200 mg/day, total fat 25-35% of calories. Mediterranean diet: high intake of olive oil, fruits, vegetables, legumes, whole grains, moderate fish and poultry, low red meat, moderate wine consumption.
